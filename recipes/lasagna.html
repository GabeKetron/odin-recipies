Lasagna

This

hearty, vegetable lasagna

is the only lasagna Sue's husband will eat. We love it!!! Hope you all enjoy as much as we do.

Time

Prep: 25 mins

Cook: 1 hr

Additional: 15 mins

Total: 1 hr 40 mins

Servings: 12

Yeild: 12 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • ¾ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¾ cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars pasta sauce
